Town of Salem 2 is a social deduction game. There are four main factions: Town, Coven, Apocalypse, and Neutrals.

Town's goal is to hang or kill every non-townie (except a select few Neutrals). Coven's goal is to be the last ones standing. Apocalypse and Neutrals have unique win conditions.

Every role has abilities that can be used at Day or Night. During the day, players can discuss during the **Discussion Phase**, vote up players to the stand in the **Voting Phase**, then decide whether to guilty them or not. At default, maximum 3 trials a day and 1 hanging.

The game usually starts with each player claiming a Town **alignment**, eg. Town Investigative (TI), Town Killing (TK), etc. Players will be proven fake or real from there.
