Town Roles:
- Town Investigative
Coroner: During the day, **Autopsy** a dead player to learn the killer's role. During the night, **examine** a player to learn if they are one of the roles you discovered through any autopsy.
Investigator: During night, **Investigate** a player. If they visited an opposing faction member (to them) at any point, you will see Trespassing. If they attacked a player that Night, you will see Murder.
Lookout: During night, **Watch** a player to see all players that visit them.
Psychic: During night, target a player to be guaranteed to be in your **Vision** that night. On odd-numbered nights, get two players, one OR MORE of which is Town, and on even numbered nights, get three players, one OR MORE of which is Evil.
Seer: During night, choose two targets. You will be told if their roles are considered Enemies or Friends.
Spy: During night, plant **Bugs** on houses. For the rest of the game, learn what Evil roles visit those houses.
Tracker: During night, **Track** a player to see who they visit.
- Town Protective
Bodyguard: **Guard** a player with Basic Defense. If they are attacked, deal a Powerful Attack back, but take the attack onto yourself.
Cleric: Cast a **Barrier** around a player, granting Powerful Defense that night.
Crusader: **Fortify** a player each night with Powerful Defense, dealing a Basic Attack to a random visitor.
Oracle: Remotely cast an **Aegis** onto a Role, granting Powerful Defense to all players with that role.
Trapper: **Build** a **Trap**. After placing it onto a player during the night, learn the roles of all visitors. If there is a harmful visit to the trapped player, protect the trapped player and deal a Powerful Attack to the visitor. If there is any visitor, the trap is destroyed and must be rebuilt.
- Town Killing
Deputy: Shoot a player during the day. If they are Town or have at least Basic Defense, the shot will miss and only you will be notified. You have only one shot. Otherwise, all players will be notified a Deputy has fired succesfully and the target has died.
Trickster: Place a **Magic Mirror** during the night. If the target is attacked, absorb the attack and learn the role you absorbed the attack of. You can then unleash the attack on another player on subsequent nights. The attack will still show as being dealt by the original role.
Veteran: You have three **Alerts**. Alert during a night to gain Basic Defense and deal a Powerful Attack to all visitors.
Vigilante: By default, **Load** a bullet into your gun. If you have at least one bullet loaded, you can **Shoot** at night, dealing a Basic Attack to a target. If you kill a Townie, lose all remaining bullets. Maximum three bullets.
- Town Support
Admirer: At start of game, learn sub-alignment of **Obsession**. Visit a target at night to learn if they are your Obsession. Upon finding Obsession, you may **Care** for them up to twice, granting you and them Powerful Defense. During day, **Bestow** a target to grant them Astral Visits, Control Immunity, Roleblock Immunity. Do this up to twice.
Amnesiac: Gain the role of the first Townie to die (without their role being hidden). If multiple Townies die at once, randomly select one with preference to Town Power roles.
Retributionist: At night, **Raise** a Town member from the dead and use their role. Get all feedback that role would get normally.
Socialite: At night, throw a **Party** on a player, causing a UO to any attempted visitors. This UO only works once per player per Socialite. You can manually make a player exempt from your UOs during the day. 
Tavern Keeper: **Drink** with your target during the night, Roleblocking them.
- Town Power
Jailor: During day, choose a person to **Jail**, Roleblocking them and causing a UO to attempted visitors. Privately speak with your target during that night, and you may Execute them with a Powerful Attack. Execute up to three times, but lose the ability to Jail if you Execute a townie.
Marshal: During day, reveal your role and begin a Tribunal, allowing 2 players to be hanged. Upon being voted up, they will be hanged instantly. The Marshal gets +1 vote. Tribunal hangs will not have their roles/wills revealed until the day is over. 2 Tribunal charges.
Mayor: During day, reveal your role and gain +1 vote. You get +1 vote every day while revealed.
Monarch: **Knight** a player at night, granting them +1 vote, and notifying the town of who the Monarch knighted. Gain Basic Defense while at least one Knight is alive. 2 Knight charges.
Prosecutor: **Prosecute** a player during the day, revealing your role and instantly hanging them. Does not count as the day's one allowed hang. 2 Prosecute charges, but lose all charges if you Prosecute a townie.

Coven Roles:
- Coven Power
Archmage: **Retrain** any Coven member into another Coven role each night, excluding Coven Killing and Coven Power. The target role cannot be a role that currently or formerly was present in the game. You can retrain yourself into any Coven role.
Hex Master: **Hex** a player at night. If all remaining enemy players are Hexed, a Hex Bomb will occur, instantly winning the game.
Witch: **Control** a player at night and choose a player to **Target**. Reveal their role to the Coven. Receive all feedback your target would have received.
- Coven Killing
Conjurer: Kill one player during the day. One charge.
Jinx: Visit a player's house, killing one visitor to that player. However, reveal your identity to all players visiting your target.
Ritualist: Attempt a **Blood Ritual** each night, guessing the role of another player. If correct, they will die. 2 charges. Town Investigatives exempt.
- Coven Deception
Dreamweaver: Visit a player at night to apply Dreamwoven if the target is Town. If the target is not visited by another Town role next night, they are **Insomniac** and cannot use any abilities. Insomnia can only be cured by the Dreamweaver's death.
Enchanter: **Enchant** a player at night to appear evil to Town Investigatives, expiring once they are visited by a Town Investigative. At night, **Alter** a player's will. If they die that night, the altered will will appear instead. 2 Alterate charges.
Illusionist: Cast an **Illusion** on a Coven member to make them appear innocent for that night. Cast a **Phantasm** to make your entire team appear innocent for one night. One Phantasm charge. You can choose a role/alignment to appear as at any time. You will appear as that role if you are killed.
Medusa: **Stone Gaze** a player at night. When they die, their will is lost and their role is hidden. 3 charges.
- Coven Utility
Necromancer: Raise a dead player at night and use their ability. You may only use Evil corpses once.
Potion Master: 2 potions: Heal potion, reveal potion. Heal potion grants Powerful Defense for that night. Reveal potion reveals a player's role to all Coven.
Voodoo Master: 3 options: Silence, blind, or deafen. Blinded players will not receive ability feedback that night. Deafened players cannot see the chat the next day but can still chat. Silenced players cannot speak in chat. If voted up, they will post "..." in the chat once.
Wildling: Visit a player to **Sense** who they visit and who visits them. Can hear all whispers.

Neutral Roles:
- Neutral Evil
All Neutral Evils have basic defense for 2 nights.
Doomsayer: Guess three players' roles at night. If you get them right, they will die the night after that, and you will leave town the next day in victory.
Executioner: Receive a Town target at the start of the game. If they are hanged, you win and leave. If they die (not by hanging), you become a Jester.
Jester: If you are hanged, you win, and the next night, you can kill anyone who didn't vote innocent on you. You must speak a certain number of messages a day.
Pirate: Receive three players' Roles at the beginning of the game as Landlubbers. Scour a player each night to reveal if they are a LL. Plunder a player to do a basic attack, but if you kill a non-LL, lose the ability to plunder. Once all three LLs are dead, leave in victory.
- Neutral Killing:
All Neutral Killings have basic defense forever.
Arsonist: Douse a player in gasoline at night, and douse all who visit you. Ignite doused targets at night to deal a Unstoppable Attack to them.
Serial Killer: Do a Basic Attack each night. Or, use **Cautious** to kill all who visit you, destroying their wills. If you kill two nights in a row, your next attack is a Rampaging Powerful Attack. Your next enhanced attack is shortened by number of players killed by this attack (eg. kill 2+ = get it back instantly).
Shroud: Do a Basic Attack each night. Or, **Shroud** a player, attacking all players they visit with a Basic Attack. The Shrouded player will be seen with Murder.
Werewolf: Deal a Rampaging Powerful Attack on nights 2, 4, and every night after. On nights 1 and 3, leave a **Scent** on a player to learn who they visit for the rest of the game.

Neutral Apocalypse:
If solo, NA will have basic defense and appear as town to TIs. All transformed NA have Unstoppable Defense (and must be hanged).
Baker: Feed 3x **Bread** to a player at night. After three alive players have Bread, transform into **Famine**.
Famine: All players without Bread will die that night. All Breaded players consume one Bread per day. Visit a Breaded player to consume one of their pieces of Bread.
Berserker: Basic Attack a player starting on night 2. After one kill, becomes Powerful. After two, becomes a rampage. After three, transform to **War**.
War: Attack two players a night with rampaging unstoppable attacks.
Plaguebearer: Infect a player with **Plague** each night. All infected players (including you) spread Plague by visiting and being visited. Upon infecting all alive players, become **Pestilence**.
Pestilence: All players start with 1 stack. Upon visiting/being visited, players spread another stack of Pestilence. Upon getting 3 stacks, die. Visit players to instantly give them 3 stacks.
Soul Collector: **Reap** a player each night, making them appear as Soul Collectors to TIs. Gain one **Soul** each night. If a Reaped player dies, get another Soul. Upon getting 7 souls, become **Death**.
Death: If you are not hanged by the end of the day, instantly win.
